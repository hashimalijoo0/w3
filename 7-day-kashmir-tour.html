7 Days Kashmir Tour

Duration: 7 Days
Theme: Leisure & Nature
Transport: Private Cab
Best Season: All Year

Experience Paradise on Earth

This comprehensive 7-day tour takes you through the most iconic destinations of Kashmir. Starting from the floating gardens of Srinagar, you'll travel to the "Meadow of Flowers" in Gulmarg, explore the pristine valleys of Pahalgam, and witness the golden peaks of Sonamarg. This itinerary is designed to give you a perfect blend of leisure, adventure, and cultural immersion. Only Snowman Adventures offers this curated experience with premium hospitality.

Brief History & Significance

Srinagar: The summer capital, founded by Emperor Ashoka, is famous for its historic Mughal Gardens and the Dal Lake. Gulmarg: Originally called 'Gaurimarg' by shepherds, it's a world-renowned ski resort and home to the world's highest golf course. Pahalgam: The 'Valley of Shepherds' serves as the base for the Amarnath Yatra and offers breathtaking scenery.

Tour Highlights

  • Shikara Ride on the iconic Dal Lake during sunset
  • Gondola Ride in Gulmarg - one of the highest cable cars in the world"
  • Saffron fields of Pampore (in season)
  • Pony ride to Baisaran Valley ("Mini Switzerland")
  • Thajiwas Glacier visit in Sonamarg
  • Stay in a traditional Kashmiri Houseboat

Day-by-Day Itinerary


Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar & Shikara Ride

Arrive at Srinagar Airport. Meet our representative and transfer to your houseboat. In the evening, enjoy a relaxing 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake. Dinner and overnight stay in Houseboat.

Day 2: Srinagar to Gulmarg (Day Trip)

Drive to Gulmarg (52 km). Enjoy the scenic drive through pine forests. Take the famous Gondola ride (Phase 1 & 2). Visit the golf course and the historic church. Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.

Day 3:Day 3: Srinagar to Pahalgam

Drive to Pahalgam (90 km). En route, visit the Saffron fields of Pampore and the Avantipur ruins. Check-in at your hotel in Pahalgam. Evening at leisure by the Lidder River.

Day 4: Day 4: Pahalgam Exploration

Full day in Pahalgam. Visit Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Chandanwari. You can also take a pony ride to Baisaran meadows. Overnight stay in Pahalgam.

Day 5: Pahalgam to Srinagar (Local Sightseeing)

Drive back to Srinagar. Visit the famous Mughal Gardens: Nishat Bagh (Garden of Pleasure), Shalimar Bagh (Abode of Love), and Chashme Shahi (Royal Spring). Visit the Shankaracharya Temple. Overnight in Srinagar.

Day 6: Srinagar to Sonamarg (Day Trip)

Full day excursion to Sonamarg ("Meadow of Gold"). Visit Thajiwas Glacier where snow remains round the year. You can enjoy snow activities. Return to Srinagar in the evening.

Day 7: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey with beautiful memories of Kashmir.
