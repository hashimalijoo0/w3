Our Story

A journey of passion, mountains, and hospitality

Welcome to Snowman Adventures. Our journey began with a simple passion: to share the breathtaking, untold beauty of Kashmir with the world. Born in the heart of the Himalayas, we grew up exploring the hidden valleys, pristine lakes, and rugged peaks that make this region a paradise on earth.

The Beginning

What started as small guided tours for friends and family quickly grew into a mission to provide authentic, safe, and unforgettable adventure experiences. We realized that many travelers were missing out on the true essence of Kashmir-the offbeat trails, the local culture, and the silent majesty of the high-altitude glaciers.

Our Philosophy

At Snowman Adventures, we believe in sustainable and responsible tourism. We are committed to preserving the fragile ecosystem of our mountains while offering top-notch services. Whether it's a challenging trek to a frozen lake or a leisurely family photography tour, we ensure every detail is taken care of, so you can immerse yourself fully in the experience.

Looking Ahead

Today, we are proud to be one of the premier adventure trek providers in the region. But our spirit remains the same-rooted in the warm hospitality of Kashmir and the spirit of adventure. We invite you to be a part of our story and create memories that will last a lifetime.
