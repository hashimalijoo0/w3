14 Days Explorer Tour

Duration: 14 Days
Route: Kashmir - Gurez - Ladakh
Transport: 4x4 SUV
Best Season: May-Sep

The Ultimate Himalayan Odyssey

For the true explorer, this 14-day grand tour offers an unparalleled journey across the diverse landscapes of the Himalayas. From the lush green valleys of Kashmir to the remote, untouched beauty of Gurez Valley, and finally ascending to the high-altitude desert of Ladakh. This isn't just a sightseeing trip; it's an expedition through culture, geology, and history.

Brief History & Significance

Gurez Valley: Historically part of the ancient Silk Route, Gurez is home to the Dard Shin tribe, whose ancestors are believed to be the original Aryans. The valley is dominated by the majestic Habba Khatoon peak. Ladakh: Often called "Little Tibet," its history is intertwined with Buddhism and trade. Monasteries perched on cliffs date back over a thousand years.

Tour Highlights

  • Explore the offbeat and pristine Gurez Valley
  • Drive across Zojila Pass, the gateway to Ladakh
  • Visit the "Moonland" landscapes of Lamayuru
  • Witness the changing colors of Pangong Tso Lake
  • Experience the culture of the Dard tribes
  • Cross Khardung La, one of the world's highest motorable passes

Day-by-Day Itinerary


Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Arrival at Srinagar. Shikara ride on Dal Lake. Overnight in Houseboat.

Day 2: Srinagar Local Sightseeing

Visit Mughal Gardens and Shankaracharya Temple. Explore the old city markets. Overnight in Hotel.

Day 3: Srinagar to Gurez Valley

Drive to Gurez via Razdan Pass (11,672 ft). Ensure permits are checked at checkpoints. Enjoy the breathtaking views of the Kishan Ganga River. Overnight in Dawar, Gurez.

Day 4: Gurez Valley Exploration

Visit Tulail Valley and Sheikhpura. See the Habba Khatoon peak and spring. Interact with the local Dard community. Overnight in Gurez.

Day 5: Gurez to Srinagar

Drive back to Srinagar from Gurez. Relax in the evening. Overnight in Srinagar.

Day 6: Srinagar to Kargil via Sonamarg

Depart for Kargil. Pass through Sonamarg and cross the treacherous Zojila Pass. Visit the Drass War Memorial. Overnight in Kargil.

Day 7: Kargil to Leh

Drive to Leh. En route visit Mulbekh Monastery, Lamayuru ("Moonland"), Magnetic Hill, and Pathar Sahib Gurudwara. Arrive in Leh.

Day 8: Leh Local Sightseeing

Acclimatization day. Visit Leh Palace, Shanti Stupa, and Hall of Fame.

Day 9: Leh to Nubra Valley

Drive via Khardung La (18,380 ft). Visit Diskit Monastery and Hunder Sand Dunes. Double-humped camel ride. Overnight in Nubra.

Day 10: Nubra to Pangong Lake

Drive to Pangong Tso via Shyok river route. Witness the mesmerizing blue lake. Overnight in camps near the lake.

Day 11: Pangong Lake to Leh

Witness sunrise at the lake. Drive back to Leh via Chang La Pass. Visit Hemis and Thiksey monasteries on the way.

Day 12: Leh to Jispa/Sarchu (or Tso Moriri Option)

Depending on preference, start the journey towards Manali or visit Tso Moriri. *For this itinerary, we assume a return to Srinagar via flight or road, so this day is a free day in Leh for shopping.*

Day 13: Free Day / Reserve Day

Buffer day for weather contingencies or relaxation.

Day 14: Departure

Transfer to Leh Airport (IXL) for flight back home.
