14 Days Kashmir Tour

Duration: 14 Days
Theme: Leisure & Nature
Transport: Private Cab
Best Season: All Year

Experience Paradise on Earth

This immersive 14-day expedition takes you across the breathtaking landscapes of Kashmir and Ladakh—two of the world’s most spectacular Himalayan regions. Beginning in the serene lakes and Mughal gardens of Srinagar, you journey through the roaring Aharbal Waterfall, the botanical charm of Kokernag, and the lush meadows of Pahalgam and Gulmarg. The adventure deepens as you explore the remote beauty of Gurez Valley before crossing the legendary Zoji La into Kargil and moving onward to the high-altitude wonders of Leh, Nubra, and Pangong. Designed for travellers seeking nature, culture, and adventure in one seamless journey, this itinerary offers a complete Himalayan experience crafted exclusively by Snowman Adventures with premium hospitality and expert guidance.

Brief History & Significance

Srinagar: Known for its ancient heritage, Srinagar was shaped by the Mughal emperors, who built the world-famous Mughal Gardens along the Dal Lake. Its houseboats and waterways preserve centuries of Kashmiri culture. Aharbal & Kokernag: Aharbal, called the Niagara of Kashmir, and Kokernag, known for its fresh springs, were significant rest points for travellers moving between Kashmir’s southern ranges. Pahalgam: Meaning the “Valley of Shepherds,” it has been a historic pastureland and the starting point of the sacred Amarnath Yatra. Gulmarg: Originally called Gaurimarg, this meadow served as a summer retreat for kings and later became a world-class ski destination and home to one of the highest cable cars on Earth. Gurez: A hidden Himalayan valley once part of the ancient Silk Route, guarded by the iconic Habba Khatoon Peak. Kargil & Leh: Kargil stands as a symbol of India’s bravery, while Leh preserves centuries-old Buddhist culture through monasteries, stupas, and the legacy of the Silk Route. Nubra & Pangong: Nubra’s desert landscape and Pangong’s high-altitude lake have long been considered sacred and strategically important, offering landscapes found nowhere else in the world.

Tour Highlights

  • Experience a sunset Shikara ride on the iconic Dal Lake
  • Witness the power of Aharbal Waterfall and the beauty of Kokernag’s springs
  • Enjoy the Gulmarg Gondola ride—one of the highest cable cars in the world
  • Explore the desert dunes of Nubra Valley and ride the double-humped camels
  • Spend a magical night beside the shimmering blue waters of Pangong Lake

Day-by-Day Itinerary


Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar & Shikara Ride

Arrive at Srinagar Airport. Meet our representative and transfer to your houseboat. In the evening, enjoy a relaxing 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake. Dinner and overnight stay in Houseboat.

Day 2: Srinagar to Aharbal Waterfall (Day Trip)

Drive to Aharbal, known as the “Niagara of Kashmir.” Spend time at the viewpoints and explore the forest trails. Return to Srinagar by evening. Overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day 3: Srinagar to Kokernag, Daksum & Pahalgam

Drive to Kokernag and visit the botanical garden and freshwater springs. Continue to Daksum Valley and enjoy the pine forests and mountain streams. Proceed to Pahalgam for check-in. Evening at leisure by the Lidder River.

Day 4: Pahalgam – Aru, Betaab & Chandanwari Tour

Full day sightseeing in Pahalgam. Visit Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Chandanwari. Enjoy scenic meadows, river views, and photography stops. Overnight stay in Pahalgam.

Day 5: Pahalgam – Baisaran (Mini Switzerland)

Take a pony ride or small trek to Baisaran Valley, popularly known as Mini Switzerland. Enjoy panoramic mountain views and lush meadows. Dinner and overnight stay in Pahalgam.

Day 6: Pahalgam to Gulmarg

Drive from Pahalgam to Gulmarg. Explore the meadows, Golf Course, and Maharaja Palace. Enjoy a peaceful evening in Gulmarg. Dinner and overnight stay in Gulmarg.

Day 7: Gulmarg Gondola Ride & Drung Waterfall → Srinagar

Enjoy the world-famous Gondola ride up to Phase 1 and Phase 2. Afterward, visit the beautiful Drung Waterfall near Tangmarg. Return to Srinagar for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 8: Srinagar to Gurez Valley

Start early and drive to Gurez Valley via the scenic Razdan Pass. Check into your hotel or homestay in Dawar. Visit the iconic Habba Khatoon Peak in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay in Gurez.

Day 9: Gurez – Tulail Valley Tour → Sonamarg

Drive deeper into Tulail Valley, passing through untouched Himalayan villages and landscapes. Return to Dawar, then continue your journey to Sonamarg. Dinner and overnight stay in Sonamarg.

Day 10: Sonamarg to Kargil (via Zoji La & Drass)

Drive across the mighty Zoji La Pass. Visit Zero Point for snow activities. Continue to Drass and explore the Kargil War Memorial. Proceed to Kargil for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 11: Kargil to Leh (Sightseeing En Route)

Drive to Leh with stops at Lamayuru Moonland, Magnetic Hill, Sangam (Indus–Zanskar Confluence), and Gurudwara Pathar Sahib. Visit the Hall of Fame Museum before reaching Leh. Dinner and overnight in Leh.

Day 12: Leh to Nubra Valley (via Khardung La Pass)

Drive over Khardung La, one of the highest motorable passes in the world. Visit Diskit Monastery and explore the Hunder Sand Dunes. Enjoy a camel ride on double-humped Bactrian camels. Dinner and overnight stay in Nubra.

Day 13: Nubra to Pangong Lake (via Shyok Route)

Drive to Pangong Lake through the scenic Shyok Valley. Arrive at the lake and enjoy its changing shades of blue. Dinner and overnight stay in a Pangong lakeside camp.

Day 14: Pangong Lake to Leh – Departure

Witness a beautiful sunrise at Pangong Lake. Drive back to Leh via Chang La Pass. Transfer to Leh Airport for your onward flight, completing your 14-day Kashmir to Ladakh journey.
