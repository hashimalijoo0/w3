Adventure Detail

Duration: 7 Days
Difficulty: Moderate
Max Altitude: 4,200m
Best Season: Jun-Sep

About This Adventure

Experience the untouched beauty of Kashmir with this trek. From alpine lakes to lush meadows, this journey takes you through some of the most breathtaking landscapes in the Himalayas. Perfect for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

Highlights

  • Witness the pristine alpine lakes.
  • Camp under the starlit Himalayan sky.
  • Walk through blooming meadows of wildflowers.
  • Experience the warm hospitality of local shepherds.

Day-by-Day Itinerary


Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Pickup from airport, transfer to houseboat. Briefing and gear check.

Day 2: Drive to Base Camp

Scenic drive to Sonamarg. Acclimatization walk.

Day 3: Trek to Nichnai

First day of trekking through pine forests and meadows.

Day 4: Nichnai to Vishansar Lake

Cross the Nichnai Pass and descend to the beautiful lake.
