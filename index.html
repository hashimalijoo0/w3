Adventure Tours in Kashmir & Ladakh with Snowman Adventures

Experience the untold beauty of Kashmir

Snowman Adventures offers best and premium trekking and adventure tour packages across Kashmir and Ladakh. We specialize in high-altitude treks, skiing expeditions, white-water rafting, paragliding, and ice climbing, led by experienced local guides.
