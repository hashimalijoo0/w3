About Snowman Adventures

Your trusted partner for Kashmir adventures

Company Overview

Snowman Adventures is one of Kashmir’s most trusted and experienced adventure travel companies, founded by veteran mountaineer Manzoor Ahmad Joo. With more than 30 years of expertise in Himalayan trekking, skiing, snow expeditions, and guided tourism, we specialize in crafting authentic and safe mountain experiences across Jammu & Kashmir.

From high-altitude treks and alpine lakes to cultural journeys and winter expeditions, Snowman Adventures blends local knowledge with professional guiding standards. Every itinerary is designed with care—balancing adventure, safety, comfort, and the true spirit of Kashmiri hospitality.

Today, Snowman Adventures stands as a leading brand in adventure tourism, known for its reliability, experienced team, sustainable practices, and deep respect for the mountains we call home.

Our Vision

"To be the most trusted and preferred adventure partner in Kashmir, recognized globally for responsible tourism and creating life-changing experiences that empower local communities."

Meet Our Team

Mr. Manzoor

Founder & MD

Mr. Manzoor Ahmad Joo began his mountain journey in the early 1990s, inspired by the Himalayan landscapes he grew up around. He completed Para Training with the NCC and underwent specialized wilderness and emergency medical courses under renowned instructor Dr. Jim Douf, strengthening his expertise in high-altitude safety and rescue. Over the last three decades, he has led more than 1,000 trekkers across the Nepal Himalayas, Kashmir Valley, Pir Panjal, and Ladakh ranges, earning a reputation for safety, professionalism, and deep mountain knowledge. In 2007, he founded Snowman Adventures, bringing his experience together to build one of Kashmir’s most trusted adventure and trekking companies.

Hashim Ali

Operations Incharge

Hashim Ali is an engineer by profession, but an adventurer by heart—perhaps a passion inherited naturally from his father, Mr. Manzoor Ahmad Joo, whose life has been dedicated to the mountains. While adventure became his father’s profession, for Hashim it became a part of his identity and genetics. Growing up around trekking routes, snow peaks, and outdoor stories, Hashim developed a deep love for exploration. He has trekked across several alpine lakes and high-altitude trails of Kashmir, experiencing the Himalayas up close. He is also a trained skier, having completed certified skiing courses from IISM (Indian Institute of Skiing & Mountaineering) and has skied down the legendary Apharwat slopes, one of the finest ski terrains in Asia.

Faik Aijaz

High-Altitude Expedition Expert

An engineer by profession but an adventurer by passion, Faik Aijaz is a dedicated mountain athlete with an exceptional record in high-altitude exploration. He has successfully climbed and summited the formidable Kolahoi and Thajiwas peaks, along with numerous other challenging Himalayan summits. Having explored over 50 alpine lakes across Kashmir, Faik carries a deep, personal connection to the mountains. He has also completed his Basic Mountaineering Course (BMC) from JIM&WS and professional skiing courses from IISM, further strengthening his technical skills and mountain proficiency. Driven by endurance, discipline, and a relentless spirit of exploration, Faik stands as one of the most seasoned and respected mountain athletes in the region.

Asgar Ali Khan

Visual Storytellers

Our Photography Team is dedicated to preserving the beauty, emotion, and raw energy of every adventure. With a deep love for the mountains and a trained artistic eye, our photographers specialize in outdoor, travel, and high-altitude photography—ensuring every moment of your journey is captured with authenticity and precision. From the golden reflections on alpine lakes to the thrill of summit moments, they document the essence of Himalayan adventure in its purest form. Equipped with modern gear and skilled in both photography and filmmaking, they create stunning visual stories that allow trekkers to relive their experience long after the journey ends. Whether it’s candid trail moments, dramatic landscapes, or professional expedition shoots, our Photography Team ensures your memories are not just recorded—but transformed into timeless visuals.
