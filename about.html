About Snowman Adventures

Your trusted partner for Kashmir adventures

Company Overview

Snowman Adventures is one of Kashmir’s most trusted and experienced adventure travel companies, founded by veteran mountaineer Manzoor Ahmad Joo. With more than 30 years of expertise in Himalayan trekking, skiing, snow expeditions, and guided tourism, we specialize in crafting authentic and safe mountain experiences across Jammu & Kashmir.

From high-altitude treks and alpine lakes to cultural journeys and winter expeditions, Snowman Adventures blends local knowledge with professional guiding standards. Every itinerary is designed with care—balancing adventure, safety, comfort, and the true spirit of Kashmiri hospitality.

Today, Snowman Adventures stands as a leading brand in adventure tourism, known for its reliability, experienced team, sustainable practices, and deep respect for the mountains we call home.

Our Vision

"To be the most trusted and preferred adventure partner in Kashmir, recognized globally for responsible tourism and creating life-changing experiences that empower local communities."

Meet Our Team

Manzoor Joo

Founder & MD

A mountain veteran with 30+ years of Himalayan experience, Manzoor Joo has led over 1,000 trekkers across Kashmir, Ladakh, and Nepal. Trained in wilderness rescue and high-altitude safety, he founded Snowman Adventures in 2007 to bring professional, safe, and unforgettable mountain experiences to the Himalayas.

Hashim Ali

Operations Incharge

An engineer with the heart of an adventurer, Hashim grew up on the trails of Kashmir. A certified skier from IISM and an experienced trekker, he ensures each expedition runs smoothly, safely, and with authentic mountain spirit
