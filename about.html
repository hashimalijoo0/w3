About Snowman Adventures

Your trusted partner for Kashmir adventures

Company Overview

Snowman Adventures is Kashmir's premier adventure tourism company, founded with a singular vision: to share the untold beauty of the Himalayas with the world. We believe that true adventure is not just about visiting a place, but immersing yourself in its soul.

Our journey began with a passion for the mountains and a commitment to safe, sustainable, and authentic travel experiences. From the hidden valleys of Gurez to the alpine Great Lakes, we curate expeditions that leave a lasting imprint on your heart. We are more than just a tour operator; we are your local companions, dedicated to showcasing the warm hospitality, rich culture, and pristine nature of Kashmir.

Our Vision

"To be the most trusted and preferred adventure partner in Kashmir, recognized globally for responsible tourism and creating life-changing experiences that empower local communities."
